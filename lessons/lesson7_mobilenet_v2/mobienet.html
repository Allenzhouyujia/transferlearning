Revolutionary Breakthrough of Depthwise Separable Convolution

The core innovation of MobileNet V2 lies in depthwise separable convolution - a brilliant technique that separates spatial and channel-wise computations, achieving dramatic efficiency gains while maintaining representational power.

Traditional Convolution

Spatial + Channel
H × W × C_in × C_out × K²

Processes spatial and channel dimensions simultaneously in a single operation

VS

Depthwise Separable

Spatial | Channel
H × W × C_in × K² + H × W × C_in × C_out

Separates spatial filtering and channel mixing into two distinct steps

Subtle Balance of Linear Bottleneck

MobileNet V2 introduces the inverted residual with linear bottleneck - a counterintuitive design that expands before contracting, and crucially uses linear activation in the final layer to preserve information flow.

Expand
1×1 Conv + ReLU6

Dimension Expansion

Expand channels by factor t (typically 6) to provide rich representation space for spatial processing

→
Depthwise
3×3 DW + ReLU6

Spatial Filtering

Process spatial information efficiently using depthwise convolution on expanded feature maps

→
Project
1×1 Conv + Linear

Linear Projection

Project to low-dimensional space using LINEAR activation to preserve manifold structure

Traditional: ReLU Bottleneck

Problem: ReLU destroys information in low-dimensional bottlenecks due to non-reversible transformations

MobileNet V2: Linear Bottleneck

Solution: Linear activation preserves information flow and enables effective residual connections
